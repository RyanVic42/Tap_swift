Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a bus pass?

Click on the "Register" link in the navigation menu, choose "Passenger" as your role, and fill in your details. Once registered, you can generate your digital bus pass.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, Airtel Money, and bank transfers for account top-ups.

How do I use my digital pass?

Simply show the QR code on your phone to the driver when boarding the bus. The driver will scan it to verify your pass.

What if I lose my phone?

Contact our support team immediately. We can help you recover your account and generate a new pass on a different device.

How do I become a driver on the platform?

Register as a driver through our registration page. Our team will review your application and provide you with access to the driver portal.
