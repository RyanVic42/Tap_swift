About Bus Pass System

The Bus Pass System is a modern digital solution designed to streamline public transportation in Kenya. Our platform connects passengers, drivers, and administrators in a seamless ecosystem that makes bus travel more efficient and convenient.

Our Mission

To revolutionize public transportation by providing a digital platform that enhances the travel experience for passengers while empowering drivers and transport operators with modern tools.

Key Features

  • Digital Bus Passes: Generate QR code-based passes for easy verification
  • Mobile Payments: Integrated M-Pesa and other payment methods
  • Route Management: Comprehensive route and schedule information
  • Real-time Updates: Live updates on bus schedules and routes
  • Reward System: Earn points for frequent travel

How It Benefits You

For Passengers:

  • No more physical tickets or cash handling
  • Easy account top-up via mobile money
  • Digital pass storage on your phone
  • Reward points for loyal customers

For Drivers:

  • Quick passenger verification with QR scanner
  • Reduced cash handling and security risks
  • Access to route and schedule information
  • Digital record keeping

For Administrators:

  • Comprehensive system oversight
  • User management and analytics
  • Route and pricing management
  • Financial reporting and insights

Technology

Our system is built using modern web technologies including Python Flask, HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. We prioritize security, reliability, and user experience in all our development efforts.
